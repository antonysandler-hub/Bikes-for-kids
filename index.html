Bike Donation Guidelines

To ensure every child receives a safe, dependable bicycle, we accept quality bikes that are in good condition or can be professionally refurbished.

Accepted

Trek, Specialized, Giant, Cannondale, Kona, Santa Cruz, and other quality bicycle brands.

Condition

Bikes should have a solid frame and be suitable for repair or immediate use.

Not Accepted

Bicycles with cracked frames, major structural damage, or those that cannot be safely restored.
